Discovery of the Week

With the explosion of the sharing economy, there's no telling what industry could be disrupted with the leveraging of technology and software. Empowering autonomy in self-motivated individuals seems to be gaining ground all over the world as we see companies like Uber, Airbnb, and Instacart enable people to share what they already own to the world. These technologies are helping to build this sense of microentrepreneurship within global communities and gives opportunities that were once intangible.

From outsourcing solar energy to renting out your yacht, there is no shortage of ideas. This week I discovered Triip. A Vietnam-based startup that empowers locals to turn into an amateur tour guide overnight. It gives tools to create tourism packages that can be sold to tourists.
